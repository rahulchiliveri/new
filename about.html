Our Story

Vintage Lane started as a weekend market stall and grew into a carefully curated online shop. We rescue garments, repair where needed, and photograph each piece with love. Our goal is to make sustainable choices beautiful and accessible.

We source locally and partner with artisans for restoration. Every purchase supports small-scale circular fashion and reduces textile waste.
